Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Board.Lore is a climbing board community app that helps climbers track their progress and connect with compatible LED systems. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and password. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Climb Logs: We store records of the climbing problems you attempt and complete, including attempts, sends, and any grades you propose.

Favorites: We store which problems you mark as favorites to personalize your experience.

Bluetooth Usage: Board.Lore uses Bluetooth to connect to LED systems. This connection is used solely to illuminate holds on your physical board. No Bluetooth data is collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Track your climbing progress
  • Display leaderboards and community statistics
  • Enable problem sharing and other community features
